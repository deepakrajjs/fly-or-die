How to Play

🎮 Controls

Use WASD keys, arrow keys, or touch controls to move your creature around the battlefield.

🍎 Eating

Eat yellow food items to grow larger and gain points. Larger creatures can eat smaller enemies!

⚔️ Combat

Avoid enemies larger than you - they'll damage you! Use strategy to grow and overcome threats.

⭐ Power-ups

Collect star-shaped power-ups for temporary abilities:
• 🔵 Speed Boost - Move faster
• 🟡 Shield - Temporary invincibility
• 🔶 Magnetism - Attract nearby food

👹 Boss Battles

Every 3 levels, face powerful boss enemies with special attacks and multiple phases!

🏆 Characters & Achievements

Unlock new characters by completing achievements. Each character has unique abilities!
